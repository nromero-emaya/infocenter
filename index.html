WORKSTATION ETIQUETTE
  • No open beverage/food containers on your desk.
  • Cellphones or electronic devices are not allowed in the Operations area.
  • BAt the end of your shift power down all devices. (Unless you share workstations)
  • Keep the work area neat. (No clutter)
ACCCESS CARDS
  • When you complete the mandatory documentation, you will be issued 2 access cards that you need to carry with you all the time.
  • Building Policy mandates that you use your access cards to access the turnstile in the lobby. If you don't have a card yet, you'll have to leave your DUI.
  • Never leave doors open and unattended.
KITCHEN ETIQUETTE
  • Tidy up appliances/Clean up after yourself.
  • Organize the fridge, avoid storing your whole lunch bag.
  • Understand the leftover rules. don't eat what's not yours.
  • Strong odor awareness.
  • Make sure you close your toppers properly.
  • Don't let food rot in the fridge.
our coffee station
Our Guidelines for uses
  • Find our coffee stations in our operation floors. They are available whenever the area's supervisor is present.
  • This is a splash-free zone. Remember to prepare your drink; first mix the dry ingredients, then fill your cups at the water cooler after.
  • No open-face containers! Make sure that your mugs/cups are compliant with EMAYA safety guidelines.
  • Be mindful of spills. A hand vacuum cleaner will always be available so you can pick up any spilled powders.
  • Special treats will be available at least once a week or on special occasions.
  • Spoons are available for measuring only. Please use drink mixers.
  • No delivery service! You are only allowed to prepare your own drinks. One mug and/or treat per person.
  • Maximum Capacity is 3 people at a time.
